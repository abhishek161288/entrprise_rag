Annual Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to annual leave based on years of service.

0 to 2 years of service: 18 days annual leave.
More than 2 years and up to 5 years: 24 days annual leave.
More than 5 years: 30 days annual leave.

Unused leave may be carried forward up to a maximum of 10 days.
Employees must submit leave requests through Workday at least 3 business days in advance.
Emergency leave requests can be approved by the reporting manager.
Public holidays are not deducted from annual leave balances.
